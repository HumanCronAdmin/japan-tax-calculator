## Part 1: Is a tax return required?
Flowchart (YES/NO split to determine):
- Only 1 company employee → No (completed by year-end adjustment)
- Yes conditions: side job > 200,000 / 2 places of employment / annual income > 20 million / medical expenses > 100,000 / home 6 municipalities + / first year of mortgage / mid-year entry
- Risk of not filing: 14.6% delinquent tax, 15-20% additional tax for not filing
