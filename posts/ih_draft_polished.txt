## From personal pain to public tool: my free Japanese tax calculator (showing exact take-home pay!) .......

Dear Indie Hackers!

I'd like to introduce you to a project I've been working on diligently: **Free Japanese Tax Calculator**.

You can see it here: 👉 **[https://humancronadmin.github.io/japan-tax-calculator/

### A problem I faced (and knew others faced as well)

I remember when I had just moved to Japan, and my excitement at getting a new job quickly turned to mild panic." What does this salary actually mean *in my pocket*?"

Japan's tax system is... Perfect. National income tax, inhabitant tax (with a unique time lag for new residents), health insurance, pension premiums, unemployment insurance... There are many. Most of the payrolls I found online were either

1. **Paid:** Require a subscription for something I felt should be transparent.
2. **Too simple:** only shows gross income tax and not the full picture of *all* deductions.
3. **Not foreign-friendly: lacks nuances such as first year resident tax delay.
4. **Difficult to use or questionable results.

What I wanted to know was, if my offer was X amount of yen, how much would actually go into my bank account *every month*? This was not just a "nice to have".
