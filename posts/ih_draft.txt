1.  **Paid:** Requiring a subscription for something I felt should be transparent.
2.  **Too simplistic:** Only showing gross income tax, not the full picture of *all* deductions.
3.  **Not foreigner-focused:** Missing nuances like the residence tax lag for the first year.
4.  **Outdated or clunky:** Hard to use or gave questionable results.
