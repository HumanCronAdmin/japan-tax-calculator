## Why This Tool?

Japanese tax calculations are confusing for foreign workers. Enter your salary and get an instant breakdown of income tax, residence tax, social insurance, and net pay.

## Features

- **Employee & freelancer modes** — Choose your employment type for accurate calculations
- **Full tax breakdown** — Income tax, residence tax, and social insurance contributions
- **Dependent & age adjustments** — Account for dependents and age-based deductions
- **Visual pie chart** — See where your money goes at a glance
- **Monthly and annual views** — Switch between monthly and yearly take-home pay

## How It Works

1. Enter your annual income
2. Set your employment type and deductions
3. See your complete tax breakdown instantly

> **Try it now:** [https://humancronadmin.github.io/japan-tax-calculator/](https://humancronadmin.github.io/japan-tax-calculator/)

## Data Sources

Tax calculations are based on rates from the [National Tax Agency (NTA)](https://www.nta.go.jp/english/).

## Privacy

- All calculations run **in your browser** — no data is sent to any server
- No cookies, no tracking accounts
- Works offline after first visit (PWA)
